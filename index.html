Healthcare Robotics

Exploring how robots improve patient care, surgery, and hospital efficiency.

About

This website is a student project about healthcare robotics. It explains key applications, benefits, and challenges, and shows how robotics supports modern healthcare.

Key Uses of Healthcare Robots

  • Robotic-assisted surgery
  • Rehabilitation and physiotherapy support
  • Hospital delivery and logistics
  • Disinfection and cleaning
  • Remote monitoring and telepresence

Why it matters

Healthcare robots can improve accuracy, reduce workload for staff, and support safer patient care. They also raise questions about cost, training, privacy, and safety standards.
